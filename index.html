Genre:

In this game, player will establish, develop, and manage their own business starting from zero to success and strive to become a business giant in Java. But the challenge in this game is that apart from managing their own business, player will also become city managers of five major cities in Java while estimating their advantages and disadvantages. There are three types of businesses available to build, they are food & beverages, furniture and petroleum mining businesses.
