My Project

GAME

LOST: The Day I Have To Change

Genre: Mystery, Puzzle, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Telling the story of a 10 year old boy named Baskara Raya who is a whiny child. He lost his father since he was 2 years old. In the game, the player will be playing as Baskara Raya (Raya) who tries to solve the mystery of why everyone is missing. Like in the genre, here Raya will be faced with a lot of puzzle that must be solved to get closer to the truth, starting from english riddles, math riddles, simple logic, making potions, and collecting objects. Players will later be accompanied by a mysterious note that can guide the player.
